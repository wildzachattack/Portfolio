Moser Consulting wanted to build an app that would enhance employee communication and allows the employees to be heard. The other summer intern and I were tasked with creating this app. When we started discussing possible solutions with our team lead, we decided that this project would be best as a phone app. We then built a mock-up of what we thought the app should look like. We built the page templates and put the demo on Invision and released the link to fellow Moser employee's to get feedback. We then took notes on the feedback we were given and started development. We had weekly meet ups with our team lead to demo our progress. This app was built for android with Java and used Google Firebase for the back end.
